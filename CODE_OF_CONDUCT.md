# Code of Conduct

KBeacon follows the CNCF community expectation of respectful, inclusive, and professional collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus review comments on code, design, documentation, and user impact.
- Be patient with new contributors.
- Help keep the community welcoming.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Threats or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces to protect the community.
